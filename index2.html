Chaos Theory

Below is a video of a simulation in which several tripple pendulums (a triple pendulum is a pendulum with a pendulum atattched at the end, and a pendulum attatched to the end of that pendulum.) are pulled back to positions so similar, it's hard to even make out there's more than one pendulum, and released. Each pendulum has a different color that trails its end.

What just happened? Why did the pendulums all seperate out so much?

Well this is a perfect example of Chaos Theory.
